Dashboard

The dashboard webpage handles the monitoring of real-time groups in Room 34. It assumes the time to reach Room 43 is 15 minutes ETA. Each group is measured in accordance to the 'time' (set to 10) parameter, which samples the occurrences of people within the robot's viewpoint.

  • Current - metric measures the number of active people within each sampled group heading to Room 43.
  • Total - metric measures the total of inactive and active people detected at Room 34.

Display

Provides an interactive component to the system, the display webpage will provide visitor directions in accordance to the social distancing capacity set for the National Gallery. At different numbers of active visitors provide different guidance according to capacity.

Data

Provides informative data on the visitor flow detected in Room 34. We sub-divide the occurrences of people by hour and by day of detection.
